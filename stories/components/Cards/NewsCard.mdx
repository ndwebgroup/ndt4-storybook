## Usage

The News Card component can be used to highlight news articles on a homepage or news section. It provides a concise summary of the article, making it easy for users to quickly understand the content and decide whether to read more.

<ul className="list--todo">
  <li>
    ### Dos
    <ul>
      <li>Use News Cards to display recent or important news articles.</li>
      <li>Include high-quality images that are relevant to the article content.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    ### Donts
    <ul>
      <li>Don't overload the card with too much text; keep the excerpt concise.</li>
      <li>Don't use News Cards for non-news content, as it may confuse users.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>
