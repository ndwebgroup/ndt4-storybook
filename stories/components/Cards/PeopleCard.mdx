## Usage

The People Card component can be used to highlight individual profiles, such as faculty members, staff, or students. It provides a structured way to present personal information and can be adapted to different contexts on a webpage.

<ul className="list--todo">
  <li>
    ### Dos
    <ul>
      <li>Use People Cards to highlight individual profiles that you want users to notice.</li>
      <li>Ensure that the images used are high-quality and relevant to the person's profile.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    ### Donts
    <ul>
      <li>Don't use People Cards for non-personal content, as it may confuse users.</li>
      <li>Don't overload the card with too much text; keep the biography concise.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>
