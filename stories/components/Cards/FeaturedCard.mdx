The Featured Card component is designed to highlight important content with a visually appealing layout. It typically includes a prominent image, title, and label, making it ideal for showcasing featured articles, faculty, or other key information on a webpage.

<Primary />

<Controls />

---

## Usage

The Featured Card component can be used to showcase important content, such as featured faculty or articles. It helps draw attention to specific items on a webpage.

<ul className="list--todo">
  <li>
    ### Dos
    <ul>
      <li>Use Featured Cards to highlight important content that you want users to notice.</li>
      <li>Ensure that the images used are high-quality and relevant to the content being featured.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>
    ### Donts
    <ul>
      <li>Don't overcrowd the card with too much text or too many images.</li>
      <li>Avoid using low-resolution images that may appear pixelated.</li>
      <li>Don't use Featured Cards for non-essential content that doesn't require emphasis.</li>
      <li>Avoid using too many Featured Cards on a single page, as this can dilute their impact.</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
</ul>
